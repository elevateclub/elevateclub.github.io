Welcome to a space where education constantly levels up.

We specialize in technology and music but also prioritize the core curriculum. In collaboration with New York Black Belt Center, we serve our community to build up leaders and champions in both mind and body for the next generation. Check out our programs to see how we can partner today!

Breeze through grade school

with consistent day over day growth in a safe and productive environment. Learning the principles of the right tools for the right job whether with the keyboard, pen, or spoken word, our students are reinforced with proper form and technique as we guide them through their homework and martial arts training in an after school setting.

Homework comes first

Taking care of every assignment from our students' day school is our top priority. We equip our students with the right tips and tricks to improve the speed and accuracy of getting their work done. By getting their day school work done sooner, we free up time to engage in our computer and music activities!

Click through either our Elementary (K-5) or Teenage (6-12) tabs for more information about our times and activities offered.

For students entering K-5th grade, our elementary after school program with a pickup offering is the perfect afternoon transition for you and your child.

Running from 3:00PM (or right after school with pickup) to 5:30PM, our program includes Taekwondo class, homework help, and a snack break that will have them entering their evening dinner and bed-time routine in the best way possible.

For students entering 6-12th grade, our teenage after school program focuses on developing our students to handle increasing academic load on top of increasing responsibility.

Running from 5:45PM to 7:30PM, our teenage program includes Taekwondo class, homework help, and a snack break.
