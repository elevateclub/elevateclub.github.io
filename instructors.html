Instructor Brian Kim

Mr. Brian Kim was born and raised in New York City. He graduated from Stuyvesant High School’s Class of 2011 and Villanova University’s Class of 2015 with a bachelor’s degree in computer engineering. His passion for teaching began early in his college education as an academic tutor for core curriculum topics such as chemistry, physics, and calculus as well as a teaching assistant for lower level computer engineering courses. Upon graduation he would transition his work to professional software engineering across multiple domains such as cybersecurity, cloud, and finance.
